The Jibaro Markup Tool respects your privacy completely. We built this Extension to be useful, not to collect data.

1. Information Collection

Jibaro Markup Tool does NOT collect, store, transmit, or share any personal information or user data.

Specifically, we do NOT collect:

  • Personal identification information
  • Browsing history
  • Web page content or annotations
  • Usage statistics
  • Analytics data
  • Cookies or tracking data

2. Data Processing

All annotation and markup processing happens entirely within your local browser. No data is ever sent to external servers or third parties.

When you use the Extension:

  • Annotations are stored locally in your browser using Chrome's storage API
  • Text highlights and notes remain on your device
  • No data leaves your computer
  • You can export your annotations locally

3. Permissions Used

The Extension requires the following Chrome permissions:

  • "activeTab" - To access the current page for creating annotations
  • "storage" - To save your annotations and preferences locally on your device
  • "contextMenus" - To add right-click menu options for quick access

These permissions are used ONLY for the core functionality of the Extension and NOT for data collection.

4. Local Storage

The Extension stores your annotations and preferences locally on your device using Chrome's storage API. This data:

  • Stays on your device
  • Is never transmitted anywhere
  • Can be cleared by uninstalling the Extension or clearing browser data

5. Third-Party Services

The Extension does NOT use any third-party services, analytics tools, or tracking software.

6. Data Security

Since we don't collect or transmit data, there is no risk of data breaches or unauthorized access to your information through our Extension.

7. Children's Privacy

The Extension does not knowingly collect information from children under 13. Since we don't collect any data at all, the Extension is safe for users of all ages.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted with an updated "Last Updated" date at the top of this page.

9. Open Source

The Extension's source code is available for review at GitHub. You can verify that no data collection occurs by examining the code.

10. Contact

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact:
